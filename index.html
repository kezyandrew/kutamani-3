About us

We started as an idea with a belief that something was missing in the world. Unfulfilled brand aspirations, that needed to be shared with a hope that, they would be made reality. At Kutamani Agency, we are a young vibrant team with a shared purpose, that believes in meeting client aspirations that will have tremendous impact on the world. Through our Brand vision, we ensure creative thinking, research, manipulation of technology, innovation and integrity impactful to brands.

Services

Application development

We aim to identify the roots of business problems and finding or designing a software solution to solve them. We offer software development consulting service, covering software ideation, recommending a suitable implementation, gathering business information and requirements, software architecture and project design, collaborating with developers (both internal and external) and coordinating the development process and its complete implementation and Testing.

Project Management

We design projects, provide expert project management advice to suit your need and conduct quality assurance on all documents to a standard that allows projects to be approved. At the core of our workflow the following key activities; reviewing current project management processes and capabilities, providing an overview of weaknesses in current processes and controls, recommendations for better processes and controls, support in the implementation of new tools, processes, and procedures and change management and transformation support

Capacity Building and Training

Training and development improves the effectiveness of organizations and the individuals and teams within them. For that reason, we ensure that learning and development take place in order to improve group and individual performance by increasing and honing skills and knowledge. We do this by following a systematic process which typically involves Training Needs Assessment, Programme design followed by Critical preview of programme, Validation, Programme implement action, Evaluation and Follow Up

Research and Data Collection

We start with research Conceptualization in order to bring research results and concepts into the real market using Social Innovation principles to various fields. Using Analytics, we are able to deliver Market, Quantitative and Qualitative research profiles. We have undertaken different assignments to provide professional support for researchers in a number of areas including: market research, funding support, bid development, strategic planning, development research and academic research.

SMS Solutions

We do sales promotions,product launch,Brand awareness and event promotion and sms alerts we make you stand out from your competitors
